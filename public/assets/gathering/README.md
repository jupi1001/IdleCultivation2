# Gathering assets

Gathering uses **two kinds of images** in two places:

- **Area images** (this folder) = the **location card** (e.g. the oak grove, the herb garden). Shown on the gathering area card.
- **Item images** (`items/` subfolder) = **wood and herb icons** in inventory and popovers. Small icons (e.g. 64Ã—64).

So e.g. `mortal-oak-grove.webp` here is the **location**; `items/item-oak-wood.webp` is the **wood item** you receive.
